Website Topic

  • What is the purpose of the site? What do you want to accomplish? What opportunity, problem, or issue is your site addressing? My site would be about music criticism, and would aggregate reviews from multiple news outlets on one platform.
  • Who is your intended audience? Be specific. Intended audience is anyone interested in music and music journalism, especially anyone who regularly reads music reviews.
  • What type of content might be included in your site? Be specific. Probably articles that each focus on a specific album or artist and give a collective opinion from popular news sites, also links to these news sites and links to stream the albums.
