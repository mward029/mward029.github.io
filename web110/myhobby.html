Painting

Ever since high school, I've enjoyed painting. I haven't painted much compared to some, but for a majority of my college career I've been studying graphic design, so making paintings is a way to be creative without feeling like I'm doing homework.

This semester, I'm taking a painting class at Braitman Studio, a studio run by Charlotte-based artist Andy Braitman. If you haven't seen his work before, be sure to check it out - he paints a lot of colorful and abstract landscapes.

I haven't painted much, but I plan to more, so stay tuned! In the meantime, you can check out most of my art on my Instagram.
