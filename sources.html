Here are the trusted sources we go to for music criticism.

Site Desc.
Album of the Year Music Reviews, Ratings, Charts, and News. Lists all the reviews and articles from other music criticism sources for each album.
Metacritic Gives a 'Metascore' to albums by taking the average of all the critic scores across the web.
MusicBrainz A Wikipedia for music - a database of music information. We like to use it for reliable album cover images as well.
AllMusic Reviews, personalized recommendations, and staff picks.
Pitchfork "The Most Trusted Voice in Music."
NPR Music National Public Radio, aka NPR. Live radio, podcasts, articles, 'Tiny Desk Concerts,' and more.
Pigeons and Planes This is a section of the pop culture-centric news site Complex. Besides the obvious music reviews, they're also known for shows like Hot Ones and Sneaker Shopping with celebrities.
SPIN Music articles and features on musicians.
Music Critic Discover new music, read album reviews, browse bands, find new recommendations and your next favorite artist.
HipHopDX Hip hop reviews and editorials.
XXL Hip-hop news, rap videos, rap music reviews, rapper interviews - "hip hop on a higher level."
Consequence of Sound Call themselves the "missing link between mainstream pop culture and the underground." Reviews and lists.
The FADER A magazine that covers style, culture, and of course music. Great cover stories and profiles on artists.
Genius An online community dedicated to annotating song lyrics. They're also known for their videos, where artists break down their own lyrics and producers show a behind-the-scenes look on how they made the song.
Drowned in Sound A webzine featuring reviews, news, interviews, and discussion forums.
The Needle Drop Reviews and a podcast, as well as home to YouTube personality and music reviewer Anthony Fantano.
Tiny Mix Tapes A music and film zine with mostly news and reviews. They're also known for their Automatic Mix Tape Generator.
